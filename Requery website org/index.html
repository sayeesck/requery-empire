About

Welcome to requery, the official complaint registration portal for students of Empire College of Science. Our platform is designed to provide a direct and efficient channel for students to voice their concerns and complaints. With requery, students can easily submit their grievances, which are then directly forwarded to our esteemed ombudsman, Mr. Sree Kumar. Our mission is to ensure that every student's voice is heard and addressed promptly, fostering a fair and supportive academic environment. Join us in making our college a better place by using requery to share your feedback and concerns. .
